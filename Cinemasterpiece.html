				  Cinemasterpiece is an online and offline cinema. You're not hearing things. You can either one-time-purchase and view the latest hits at the comfort
                  of your home on your devices like your own cinema, or you can book a ticket online to view the latest hits in a comfy chair in the cinema. With
                  this new experience, it's now possible for both possibilities to occur.



                  With Cinemamasterpiece, a whole bunch of fresh movies are available for viewing at the time of their screenings in your nearest cinema.
                  To use Cinemasterpiece, pick the movie you like to watch, then book a time it's screening, and pick the option to digitally watch it
                  or book a seat in the cinema to view it there. And that's it. You can view the movie at home or your cinema! So go on and have a blast
                  with Cinemasterpiece!
